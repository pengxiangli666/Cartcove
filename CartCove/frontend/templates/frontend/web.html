How to Use this App

Welcome to our app! Here's a simple guide to get you started:

  1. 1. Register an account: Click the "Register" button at the top right corner of the homepage, fill in the necessary information to complete the registration.
  2. 2. Search for products: Enter keywords of the products you're interested in into the search bar, and press the "Search" button.
  3. 3. Add to cart: On the product page, find the products you like and click the "Add to Cart" button.
  4. 4. Checkout process: Go to the cart page, review your order, and click the "Checkout" button to complete the payment.
